About Me

Innovating & Stabilizing Connected Technologies Where Automation Meets Quality to Deliver Uninterrupted Digital Experiences.

Hi there! Thanks for visiting my website. I used to be an IT Support Professional with a passion for transforming technical challenges into seamless solutions, focusing on making your digital experience smooth and efficient.

Now, as a Software Quality Assurance Professional, I contribute to ensuring the perfection of digital connection experiences provided by cutting-edge technology. My passion for understanding and optimizing complex systems has deepened through over 10 years of diverse experience spanning IT system management, production line automation, mobile telecommunications network testing, and embedded software development. My logical thinking, honed by my background in mathematics, is applied daily to dissect complex IT and QA issues, design test cases, and identify root causes. Specifically, I have revolutionized operational efficiency and product reliability through Python-based PLC automation and the implementation of predictive maintenance strategies. Through my contract Field Test Engineer experience with Samsung Electronics, I gained in-depth knowledge of 5G/LTE network technologies and specialized diagnostic tools like QXDM, ensuring mobile service quality on the front lines. Furthermore, my active participation in User Acceptance Testing (UAT) and experience mentoring junior team members and conducting code reviews demonstrate my dedication to strengthening team capabilities and enhancing quality through collaboration.

My goal isn't just to 'fix' problems; it's to be a 'top-tier Quality Assurance Professional' – someone who continuously learns, adapts to rapidly changing technological environments, and delivers the highest standard of quality to help users achieve their goals. I invite you to explore my experiences and projects in more detail to see how I contribute to innovating user experiences through technical excellence.
